My Favorite Recipe:

Jollof Rice

The best rice in Africa

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Rice
  • Red bell pepper
  • Small chilli pepper
  • Onions
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Meat
  • Tomato Paste

Directions:

  1. Wash and blend Tomatoes, Pepper, Red bell pepper,Small chilli pepper and Onions together till it gets smooth.
  2. Parboil rice for about 10 minutes, then rinse and drain.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a pot and chop onions
  4. Add Tomato Paste and chopped onions to vegetable oil
  5. Allow to fry for 7 minuites.
  6. Pour blended paste into the fried mix. Add seasoning cubes and Salt.
  7. Cook until paste thickens.
  8. Add rice, Mix the rice and paste ,cook until rice is well done.
  9. Serve with Apple Fruit Juice and Enjoy!

Why is this my favorite recipe:

Jollof Rice is my favorite recipe because it is a simple dish that reminds me of home. The combination of flavors and spices always tend to hit the spot.
